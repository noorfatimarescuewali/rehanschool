Rehan School Curriculum

A blend of AI-enabled learning, entrepreneurship, soft skills and practical projects. Students pick a Sustainable Development Goal, create projects, interview experts, and build an online presence while learning to earn.

Core Modules

Digital Literacy & Internet Skills

YouTube, social media for learning, online research, content creation.

Programming & AI Tools

Intro to coding with AI, building small web apps and chatbots.

Entrepreneurship & Earning

Create projects that can earn; basic freelancing, service startups.

Communication & Leadership

Interviewing experts, public speaking, teamwork and project management.

Projects & Assessments

Every student completes an SDG project, a portfolio site, and a public presentation. Assessment is project-based and mentorship-driven.

  • SDG exploration & report
  • Portfolio & YouTube project
  • Freelance mini-gigs

Why this Curriculum?

Designed to be compact, practical and internet-native — enabling students to learn useful skills fast and apply them in the real world.

Quick Facts

Duration: Ongoing modular program · Age: 10+ · Mode: On-site & virtual
